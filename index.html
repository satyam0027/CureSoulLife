The Life Decoding Framework

Human life understood through five core dimensions - each interlocked, each essential.

Mind

The seat of thought, belief, and unconscious conditioning that shapes all experience.

Body

The physical vessel that holds trauma, memory, and the wisdom of lived experience.

Energy

The pranic field governing vitality, emotion, and the flow of consciousness.

Karma

Patterns carried across time - the invisible architecture of cause and consequence.

Consciousness

The awareness behind all experience - the source, the witness, and the destination.
